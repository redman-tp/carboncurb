What We Do

At Carbon Curb Innovations, we partner with countries to help them meet their climate change commitments by aligning policies and actions with global goals such as those outlined in the UN Framework and Paris Agreement. Our work focuses on advancing sustainable practices, reducing greenhouse gas emissions, and fostering renewable energy adoption to address the pressing challenges of global warming.

We also collaborate with organizations to empower their employees to adopt climate-friendly practices. Through tailored programs and initiatives, we enable individuals to understand their impact on the environment, adopt sustainable habits, and contribute meaningfully to collective climate action. Together, we are creating a culture of accountability and resilience in the face of climate change.

What is Carbon Curb?

Carbon Curb is a gamified B2B platform that empowers businesses to reduce their carbon footprint while engaging their employees in the process. The app provides a unique, interactive approach to sustainability, turning eco-friendly tasks into fun challenges and rewarding progress. It helps businesses track, measure, and reduce their environmental impact, while motivating teams to adopt sustainable practices through real-time feedback and rewards.

How does the gamification work?

Carbon Curb incorporates gamification to make sustainability more engaging for employees. Businesses set up sustainability challenges based on goals such as reducing energy consumption, minimizing waste, or using eco-friendly resources. Employees can participate in these tasks to earn points, level up, and unlock rewards. This not only boosts employee involvement but also fosters a company-wide culture of sustainability. Detailed leaderboards and progress tracking ensure transparency and motivate teams to compete in a friendly, impactful way.

Is Carbon Curb a paid service?

Yes, Carbon Curb is a subscription-based platform designed for businesses looking to take their sustainability efforts to the next level. Our pricing is based on the size of your organization and the features you need. We offer flexible plans to accommodate businesses of all sizes—from small startups to large enterprises. Our team is happy to discuss your specific needs and tailor a plan that aligns with your sustainability objectives.

How do I get started with Carbon Curb?

Getting started with Carbon Curb is quick and simple. First, schedule a demo with our team to explore the app’s features and see how it can work for your business. Once you're ready, select a subscription plan that best fits your company's needs. Our implementation team will assist you in setting up your profile, creating customized challenges, and engaging employees. From there, you’ll be able to track progress, celebrate milestones, and continuously improve your company’s sustainability performance.

Is Carbon Curb suitable for large organizations with 500+ employees?

Carbon Curb is specifically designed to meet the needs of larger organizations with 500+ employees. Our platform is scalable and flexible, offering tailored solutions to help businesses drive sustainability initiatives across teams of all sizes. Whether your company is looking to optimize existing green goals or implement new, measurable actions, Carbon Curb provides the tools necessary to reduce your carbon footprint, enhance employee engagement, and foster a culture of sustainability across your entire organization.
